How It Works

Step Child Experience Technology Under the Hood
1. Calibration Child dons Muse S Athena; 90-second baseline and artifact check. Auto noise detection & motion/blink rejection.
2. Play Session (20–30 min) Mini-games run while neurofeedback adjusts in real time. Local EEG processing (≤100 ms latency).
3. Reinforcement Hub Coins are spent on feeding, grooming, and upgrading virtual chickens. Reward economy tuned for ≥90% session completion.
4. Adaptive Loop EEG metrics, gameplay stats, and weekly parent ATEC ratings inform the next session. LLM selects targets; ML updates thresholds.

Frequency: three sessions per week over a 10- to 14-week course.

Gameplay Elements

Focus Mechanic Purpose
Attention Training Coin collection & obstacle avoidance with difficulty that scales to performance. Builds sustained attention and response control.
Visual Feedback Dynamic vignette shading tied to EEG regulation. Provides immediate operant conditioning cues.
Engagement System Chicken care, customization, and long-term progression. Maintains motivation across the full regimen.
