About NeurOS Platforms

Pioneering accessible, adaptive neurofeedback for children with autism—and beyond.

Our Mission

Make evidence-based neurofeedback accessible and affordable by pairing AI-driven personalization with engaging gameplay that keeps children motivated.

Our Vision

Grow a platform from ASD neurofeedback to ADHD, anxiety, depression, and consumer wellness while preserving rigor and affordability.

Hear from Our Founders

Why NeurOS

Traditional neurofeedback is costly, time-intensive (20–40 clinic visits), and sees high dropout rates. NeurOS Platforms was founded in July 2025 to remove those barriers with consumer EEG hardware, an AI-driven personalization stack, and game mechanics that keep kids engaged while real-time protocols run passively.

Accessible Hardware

Consumer-grade Muse S Athena headset replaces clinic-only equipment.

AI Personalization

LLM sets macro protocol logic; ML fine-tunes thresholds each session.

Engagement Loop

Virtual chickens, care tasks, and upgrades sustain motivation.

Cost Compression

Targeting under $700 per regimen vs $4k–$8k in-clinic.

Dual Setting Design

Runs at home or in classrooms with minimal staff time.

Data Fusion

Behavioral scales, EEG metrics, and gameplay data drive adaptive decisions.

Therapist Surrogacy

Software emulates key adjustments a practitioner would make.

Scalable Pipeline

AI-first infrastructure lets us expand rapidly to new indications.

Founders

Colin Fausnaught – Co-Founder & CTO

Colin has spent more than a decade advancing brain-computer interfaces and applied AI. An early intern at OpenBCI, he co-founded RIT's first student BCI research team, earned a "Maker of Merit" award at Maker Faire Dubai, and later taught a Brain-Computer Interfacing course at RIT. He also taught neurotech on his YouTube channel The BCI Guys.

At NeurOS he architects the adaptive neurofeedback engine, safeguards real-time signal integrity, and steers protocol expansion for future therapeutic indications.

Nicholas Merten – Co-Founder & CEO

Nicholas previously founded Digifox, an all-in-one finance app integrating interest earning, asset investing, and P2P transfers that grew to more than 20,000 users. He also launched one of the largest finance channels on YouTube, amassing over 500,000 subscribers.

At NeurOS he focuses on school partnerships, parent and stakeholder education, product strategy, and capital planning, ensuring NeurOS can bring safe, research-backed neurofeedback to those who need it most.
